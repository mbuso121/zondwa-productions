About Zondwa Productions

Zondwa Productions has been working in all the provinces of South Africa since 2006, performing powerful, interactive and exciting Industrial Theatre Shows and Branded Theatre for different companies in their workplaces.

Our live shows educate, engage, inform, and drive real behavior change for companies in all industries. We ensure that we engage our audiences in creative ways that resonate in their minds for the longest time.

From cooperatives awareness campaigns, participative team building facilitation experiences, and community/public education we help companies communicate important workplace messages in a way that is fun, engaging, and unforgettable.

At Zondwa Productions, we create customized live theatre plays that address topics such as:

  • Fraud and Anti-Corruption
  • Occupational Health and Safety
  • Financial Literacy
  • Employee Wellness
  • Gender-Based Violence
  • Disability Awareness
  • Alcohol and Substance Abuse
  • And more tailored topics

We work with professional performers and actors, delivering shows in languages preferred by the client.

Why Industrial Theatre

Industrial Theatre transforms complex or sensitive corporate messages into memorable experiences, turning passive training into active engagement.

It creates a shared emotional experience between workers and management, fostering dialogue and supporting organizational transformation.

Workers retain significantly more information through performance compared to traditional methods, and feel inspired to act on the messages presented.

It is far more engaging and interactive than videos, pamphlets, or lectures.

What We Do

Branded Theatre

Zondwa Productions specializes in branded theatre, creating live branded communication performances. We turn company products and messages into engaging entertainment through live brand activations.

We transform retail spaces, product launches, and marketing campaigns into powerful storytelling experiences.

Our performances take place in shopping centres, taxi ranks, train stations, and more.
